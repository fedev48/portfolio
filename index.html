Who am I ?

A Unity developer / Technical sound artist

I'm a Unity developer with expertise in C#. My journey into game development began with music composition and sound design. My exploration of FMOD during university led me to study programming to fully grasp audio implementation. After completing my studies, I was offered the opportunity to work as a programmer, alternating between coding and audio-related tasks.

My Expertise

Unity Development

I've had the opportunity to work on projects of varying scopes, participating in the development from the alpha stage through to final release as a programmer.

Sound Design

I have experience in both game development and sound design for media. I can generate foley, dialogue recording, and synthetic sound design. Additionally, I can build FMOD projects and implement audio within the engine.

Music Composition

Since childhood, I have been passionate about music, which inspired me to study at a conservatory and pursue music production. I am skilled in composing a wide range of music, from orchestral to electronic, including both traditional linear and adaptive compositions.
